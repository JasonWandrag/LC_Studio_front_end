Diversity is key in the workplace, LC Studio offers your business the opportunity to work with high potential junior developers that can add an edge to your staffing portfolio.

Junior Developers Journey

Young people (18-26 years old) from previously disadvantages communities, join a 12-months hands-on coding bootcamp at Life Choices Academy.

First Six-Months

Classroom based, youth are exposed to modules on technical infrastructure, software basics, business programming, basic design structure, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python and MySQL. Besides technical modules, youth also undergo personal & professional development trainings.

For more detail on the academy please visit our site

During the second 6-months, youth move in LC Studio departments. They engage with real projects, clients and deadlines offering them real world experience.
