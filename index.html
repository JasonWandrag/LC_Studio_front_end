A unique development agency with a bonus

We pride ourselves in delivering high quality tech solutions at competitive costs, while transforming South Africa

How we do this

We are a social enterprise owned by a not-for-profit organisation. Our team is made of a handful of senior developers and experienced freelancers working with a large pool of interns. While working in the agency, students gain real experience, diversify their skills set and get promoted to industry. We pride ourselves for assisting institutions in their digital conquest whilst assisting young people to find meaningful jobs.
